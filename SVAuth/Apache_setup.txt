Open the httpd.conf, then searh the <IfModule alias_module> tag and insert the two lines.
"/....../" is the parent directory of your SVAuth installtion.

<IfModule alias_module>
#For SVAuth
    Alias /SVAuth/ "/......../SVAuth/SVAuth/"
</IfModule>
<Directory "/......../SVAuth/SVAuth/">
    Options Indexes FollowSymLinks
    AllowOverride None
    Require all granted
</Directory>


Depending on the OS, the way to restart apache can be:
sudo /opt/bitnami/ctlscript.sh restart apache
or
sudo service apache2 restart
